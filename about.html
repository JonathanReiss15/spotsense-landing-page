About SpotSense

We (humans) are addicted to screen based thinking—the idea that everything needs to be controlled either through our phones or computers.

Screen based thinking turned a light switch into an app where you have to:

  1. Unlock your phone
  2. Go to the home screen
  3. Find the app
  4. Wait for it to load
  5. Find the device you want to turn on
  6. Hit the power button
We have to end screen based thinking.

Our mission is to empower developers to build experiences that are so in tune with the real world, their users think it’s magic.
